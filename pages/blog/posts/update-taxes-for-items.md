As of **22nd November 2025**, the Government of India has revised GST rates for many items. This means item tax details need to be updated. Instead of updating each item one by one (which can take hours!), you can update all items under the same HSN code at once using the **Update Taxes for Items** feature in *India Compliance*.

This guide will show you how to do it in just 4 simple steps.

## Why Use This Feature?

Whether due to GST 2.0 updates, periodic tax rate revisions, or changes in your business requirements, you may need to update item taxes from time to time. Instead of manually editing each item, this feature allows you to:

- Update all items linked to the same HSN code in one go
- Save significant time and effort compared to manual updates
- Ensure consistency and accuracy across all items sharing the same HSN code

## How to Update Taxes in 4 Simple Steps

### Step 1: Find Your HSN Code

- Go to the **GST HSN Code** list and identify the HSN code with revised tax rates under GST 2.0, or locate the code for any item whose taxes you want to update.
- Click on the HSN code to open it.

### Step 2: Update the Tax Rates

Update the taxes as per the requirements.

#### For New Tax Rates
- Add a new row with the revised rate
- Enter **today’s date** (or your chosen effective date) in the `Valid From` field
- This ensures the new rate is applied from that date onwards

### Step 3: Save the Document

Click **Save** to apply your changes.

### Step 4: Update All Items at Once

- Click the **"Update Taxes for Items"** button.
- All items linked to this HSN code will be updated automatically.

## Summary

With the **Update Taxes for Items** feature, keeping your item taxes accurate is quick and simple:

1. **Find** your HSN Code
2. **Update** the tax rates
3. **Save** the document
4. **Click** "Update Taxes for Items"

This saves hours of manual effort and ensures all your items always reflect the correct tax rates—whether due to GST 2.0 or any other tax revision.
